Who We Are

     Baked into our fabric as a brand is sustainability; both environmental and social. We use biodegradable materials, recycled plastics, and materials that require less water to produce.


     Beyond ecofriendly materials, we pay workers involved in manufacturing and supply real living wages. We aim to always be fair and forward thinking so that we can expect that this brand can serve humanity for a long time.

Ethos

     Our goal is to make clothes that people will be able to enjoy for a long time. We do not want to overproduce as in fast fashion. We want to raise awareness to environmental issues and dangers to ecosystems and inspire conscious choices in the way that we live our lives.


     The choices we make go beyond clothing and reach all aspects of life like how much water we use, how much of which products we buy (and why we buy). We believe that everyone should be interested in sustaining the earth, ecosystems, and resources that make life beautiful for all humans.

Our Style

     Every piece is based on a plant or animal. Our goal is to capture the functional features of every plant and animal in the performance of our clothing. We want to mix the craft, detail, and artistic nature of haute couture with the high performance, function, and modern appeal of techwear.
